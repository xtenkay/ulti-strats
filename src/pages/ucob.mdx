- [**Fellruin**](#bahamut): Spread around middle > Neuro opposite of Bahamut

- [**Tenstrike**](#bahamut): 1 is safe spot, use marker for second set positions
- [**Octet**](#bahamut): Tank LB3, Arms Length Twin Dive for safety (Dive goes left of Twin ideally)
- [**Adds**](#adds): Bosses tanked between 1 and 3
- [**Golden**](#golden-bahamut): Standard, Tank LB if not full 8 stack
</Card>

<h5>BiS Notes</h5>
- Any gear at or above 470 will have their substats capped.
- Relic weapons will have their substats capped at **127**.
- The lowest potions you can use and still get maximum benefits are HQ Grade 3 Tinctures.

## Golden Bahamut ##
    - <a href="https://raidplan.io/plan/Y4WRFLhdHSQ7oLN_" target="_blank">Golden Raidplan</a> <sl-badge variant="success">New!</sl-badge>
    - Akh Morn order - Share > MT invuln > OT invuln > Share
    - Shared busters are kitchen sinked with all available mits
    - 90s target mit has to be used first GCD to be up for Morn Afahs 3 and 5!
    - 60s/90s CD AoE mit - Either Morn Afahs 1/3/5 or 2/4
    - 2m CD AoE mit - Either Morn Afahs 1/4 or 2/5
    - NO tank LBs in default mit plan unless it's an emergency (e.g 7- person stack or invuln not up)
<Notification title="Second Set">
    The second set of mechanics in Golden(after the first Exas) swaps the order of the mechanics into Buster > Stack, make sure you dont put the buster on the party!<br></br>
    This swap is only for this set!
</Notification>
<Notification title="Mitigation Timing">
    Catching the 1/3/5 stacks with 90s (Addle/Feint) mit can be hard for newer players to the phase, make sure to communicate if you mess up the timing so that the party can adjust by Tank LB'ing or using extra mit!
</Notification>

## Marker Usage ##
The following assumes the use of the **Modified Aether PF** markers.

<code>123</code> are used for Neurolink placement.

<code>D</code> is used for the knockback on phase transitions like Nael and Bahamut.

<code>ABC</code> are used for Nael Divebombs

<code>4</code> is used for the Stack during Adds phase.
