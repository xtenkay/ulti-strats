## Tips ##
<Notification title="Supression Light Pillar">
  If you are baiting the Light Pillar with the Mistrals (Green Cleaves) make sure to wait until the Garuda green AoE goes off before you walk into it or you will die and the pillar will go on a random party member.
</Notification>

<Notification type="primary" title="LB Cheese Transition">
SGE: 
1) After melee LB, eukprog + kerachole
2) After Garuda gets chomped, Zoe EukProg
3) When screen goes white, Kerachole

SCH:
1) After melee LB, succor, (optional) fairy on steady to stop Embraces
2) As soon as garuda gets pulled in to be eaten, Fey Illumination and start fishing for a critlo and deploy it
3) If you don't get a critlo, soil as screen is going white (you can do this soil everytime independently of critlo aswell)

WAR:
1) Pop Veng, Thrill, Raw on Garuda crunch
2) Before buffs time out hit Shake it Off

PLD:
1) Hit Divine Veil after Garuda crunch

GNB:
1) Hit Heart of Light when screen goes white after Garuda crunch
</Notification>
