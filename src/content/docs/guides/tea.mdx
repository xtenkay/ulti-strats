### Perfect Alexander ###
- <a href="https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=170875560147661&preview=1" target="_blank">**Toolbox**</a>
:::tip[Stillness]
You have time to move after Stillness/Motion resolves and before the forced march, prioritize doing them right over getting to your spot!
:::
:::caution[Light Beacon]
You can be at the wall as Light Beacon and it wont kill people. The only failure condition for this mech is touching the beacon!

Please do this as it removes the chance of someone being too close to light and dying!
:::
:::note[Fate Cal Beta]
If you are one of the Dark Debuff players make sure to go towards the wall to bait your jump before doing the stack/spread!
:::

### Marker Usage ###

<code>143</code> are used for Trines during Perfect Alexander.

<code>2</code> is used for the far tether during Timestop.

<code>A</code> is used to indicate North.

<code>B</code> is used for the second and third water stacks during BJ/CC.

<code>C</code> is used for the Dark Beacon position during Final Word on Perfect Alexander.

<code>D</code> is used for the first water stack in BJ/CC.

### Frequently Asked Questions ###

- <h6>I didnt press anything during Stillness and still died?</h6>
- You need to fully detarget the boss for Stillness, if you only stop pressing your GCDs you risk auto attacking the boss and triggering the mechanic, this means you should press Escape (Or Circle on PS controller) TWICE not once!
