### Summary ###
- [**Beetle**](#beetle): Light Party Prio - H > R > M > T
- [**M&F**](#mf): BPOG - GPOB, Party SW for Limitless
- [**Final Omega**](#final-omega): West side vertical conga for monitors
- [**Blue Screen**](#blue-screen): Melee Flex
- [**Dynamis Delta**](#delta): Blues Beetle, Christmas closest to Beetle breaks first
- [**Dynamis Sigma**](#sigma): Macro Towers, AM Dynamis
- [**Dynamis Omega**](#omega): AM for both sets
- [**Alpha Omega**](#alpha-omega): True Clock Proteans, MT and Prange swap for Meteor, Healers talk about who goes mid (Shield Healer is better middle)

<h5>BiS Notes</h5>
- Mix of i630 (Abyssos) gear, and i635 gear from The Lunar Subterrane.
- Endwalker Relic Weapons are BiS and have their substats capped at **287**.
